Balsamic Bruschetta

Bruschetta an Italian Appetizer

It starts with a base of toasted or grilled bread. The bread is then served warm either with a topping spooned over or with a topping on the side so each person can build their own bruschetta. Bruschetta is meant to be served as a hand-held finger food, large enough for one or two bites.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French bread
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Parmeson Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Black Peppper
